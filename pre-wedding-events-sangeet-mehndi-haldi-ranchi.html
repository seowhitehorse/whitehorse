Roka / Sagai, The Official Beginning

🕉️ Tradition & Meaning

The Roka (also called Sagai or engagement) is the formal announcement that two families have agreed to unite through marriage. It's an intimate ceremony where elders from both sides bless the couple, exchange gifts, and "stop" (roka) the bride and groom from considering other alliances. In Jharkhand, this ceremony often includes traditional rituals specific to the family's community, whether Bihari, Bengali, Marwari, or tribal heritage.

📅 When & Duration

Typically held 3-6 months before the wedding, the Roka is a 2-3 hour ceremony usually hosted at the bride's home, a banquet hall, or a family temple. In Ranchi, popular venues include intimate spaces at Radisson Blu, Capitol Hill, or private farmhouses in Namkum and Kanke.

🎨 Decor Ideas for 2026

  • • Floral Elegance: Marigold and rose arrangements with traditional brass diyas and Sohrai art-inspired backdrops
  • • Minimalist Chic: Pastel drapes, fairy lights, and personalized signage with the couple's names in Devanagari script
  • • Jharkhand Touch: Incorporate tribal motifs, bamboo installations, and locally sourced terracotta elements
  • • Photo Corner: Create an Instagram-worthy backdrop with family heirlooms, vintage frames, and fresh flowers

🎭 Entertainment & Atmosphere

The Roka is intimate and family-focused. Background music should be soft, classical instrumental, devotional songs, or light Bollywood melodies. Some families in Ranchi now include a short speech or toast session where parents and elders share blessings and stories.

👗 What to Wear

Bride: Pastel or jewel-toned saree, lehenga, or Anarkali suit. Popular choices in Ranchi include Banarasi silk, Chanderi, or contemporary Indo-western fusion wear.
Groom: Kurta-pajama, sherwani, or formal Indo-western outfit in coordinating colors.
Families: Traditional ethnic wear, sarees, salwar suits, kurtas, and sherwanis in elegant, coordinated palettes.

✅ Planning Checklist

  • ☐ Finalize guest list (typically 30-80 close family members)
  • ☐ Book venue or prepare home for hosting
  • ☐ Arrange pandit/priest for rituals

Mehndi Ceremony, Art, Tradition, and Bonding

🕉️ Tradition & Meaning

The Mehndi ceremony is a celebration of beauty, blessing, and female bonding. Intricate henna designs are applied to the bride's hands and feet, symbolizing love, prosperity, and the auspiciousness of marriage. It's a time when women from both families come together, singing traditional songs, sharing stories, and blessing the bride as she prepares for her new journey.

In Jharkhand, Mehndi ceremonies often blend North Indian traditions with local customs. Families may include Nagpuri folk songs, tribal dance performances, or regional sweets like thekua and tilkut as part of the celebration.

📅 When & Duration

Typically held 1-2 days before the wedding, the Mehndi ceremony lasts 3-5 hours. Traditionally an afternoon or early evening event, the 2026 trend in Ranchi is shifting toward Mehndi brunches, daytime celebrations with natural light, fresh florals, and a relaxed garden-party vibe.

🎨 Decor Ideas for 2026

  • • Marigold Magic: Ceiling installations, floral curtains, and genda phool backdrops, quintessentially Indian and perfect for Ranchi's climate
  • • Rajasthani Vibes: Low seating with colorful cushions, umbrellas, puppets, and mirror-work fabrics
  • • Sohrai Art Backdrop: Commission local Jharkhand artists to create stunning Sohrai-inspired murals or panels as photo backdrops
  • • Boho Garden Setup: Macramé hangings, dreamcatchers, pastel drapes, and wildflower arrangements for a modern, Pinterest-worthy aesthetic
  • • Mehndi Stations: Beautifully decorated seating areas with cushions, low tables, and personalized signage

🎭 Entertainment & Atmosphere

The Mehndi ceremony thrives on music, laughter, and participation. Live dhol players, folk singers performing Nagpuri or Bhojpuri songs, and Bollywood playlists create an energetic, joyful atmosphere. Many families in Ranchi now hire professional emcees to organize games, dance-offs, and interactive activities that keep guests engaged.

Popular Entertainment Ideas:

  • • Live dhol and Nagpuri folk music performances
  • • Mehndi design competition among guests
  • • Traditional dance performances (Chhau, Jhumar, Paika)
  • • Bollywood dance medleys by friends and family
  • • Photo booth with props and instant printing
  • • Mehendi artist stations for all guests

👗 What to Wear

Bride: Bright, vibrant colors, green, yellow, orange, pink, or turquoise. Popular choices include lehengas, Anarkali suits, or sharara sets with mirror work and embroidery. Floral jewelry (gajra, marigold accessories) is a must.
Groom: Kurta-pajama or Indo-western outfit in coordinating colors. Many grooms in Ranchi opt for pastel or white kurtas with colorful Nehru jackets.
Guests: Colorful ethnic wear, lehengas, sarees, salwar suits, kurtas. The Mehndi dress code is all about vibrant, festive colors.

✅ Planning Checklist

  • ☐ Book professional mehndi artists (1 artist per 15-20 guests)
  • ☐ Finalize venue, home, banquet hall, garden, or farmhouse
  • ☐ Plan decor theme and color palette
  • ☐ Arrange seating, low cushions, chairs, or mixed setup
  • ☐ Hire entertainment, dhol players, folk singers, DJ
  • ☐ Organize catering, light snacks, chaat, sweets, beverages
  • ☐ Set up photo booth and backdrop
  • ☐ Prepare welcome drinks and refreshments
  • ☐ Coordinate outfits for bride, groom, and family
  • ☐ Arrange transportation and parking for guests
  • ☐ Hire photographer/videographer
  • ☐ Plan games and activities to keep guests engaged

Sangeet Night, Music, Dance, and Pure Joy

🕉️ Tradition & Meaning

The Sangeet (literally "music together") is the most anticipated pre-wedding event, a night of music, dance, laughter, and celebration where both families come together to perform, compete, and create memories. What began as a simple gathering of women singing folk songs has evolved into elaborate, choreographed performances rivaling professional stage shows.

For couples in Ranchi, the Sangeet is where personalities shine, where the shy uncle surprises everyone with a dance, where cousins perform hilarious skits, and where the bride and groom's love story is celebrated through music and movement.

📅 When & Duration

Held 1-2 days before the wedding, the Sangeet typically runs 4-6 hours in the evening. It's the longest and most energy-intensive pre-wedding event, often starting around 7 PM and continuing past midnight.

🎨 Decor Ideas for 2026

  • • Bollywood Glam: Stage setup with LED screens, dramatic lighting, and Bollywood-themed backdrops
  • • Starry Night: Fairy lights, chandeliers, and ceiling drapes creating a magical, romantic ambiance
  • • Jharkhand Fusion: Blend traditional tribal art with modern stage design, Sohrai patterns on LED screens, bamboo installations with contemporary lighting
  • • Dance Floor: Professional stage with sound system, lighting effects, and ample space for performances
  • • Lounge Areas: Comfortable seating zones for guests who want to relax between performances

🎭 Entertainment & Atmosphere

The Sangeet is all about performances. Both families prepare choreographed dances, skits, and musical acts weeks in advance. Professional choreographers are now hired by 70% of urban couples in Ranchi to create polished, entertaining performances.

Sangeet Performance Ideas:

  • • Bride's side vs Groom's side dance battle
  • • Couple's romantic dance performance
  • • Parents and elders' surprise performance
  • • Friends' comedy skit about the couple's love story
  • • Live band or DJ for open dance floor
  • • Fusion performances blending Bollywood with Nagpuri folk or Chhau dance
  • • Professional dancers or celebrity performers (for luxury weddings)
  • • Interactive games and audience participation segments

👗 What to Wear

Bride: Glamorous, statement-making outfit, heavily embellished lehenga, gown, or Indo-western fusion wear in bold colors like royal blue, emerald green, or fuchsia pink. Comfort is key since you'll be dancing.
Groom: Sherwani, bandhgala, or designer Indo-western suit. Many grooms coordinate colors with the bride for couple photos.
Guests: Festive, party-ready ethnic or fusion wear. The Sangeet is the most glamorous pre-wedding event, so guests dress to impress.

✅ Planning Checklist

  • ☐ Book venue with stage and dance floor (banquet halls, resorts, farmhouses)
  • ☐ Hire professional choreographer 4-6 weeks in advance
  • ☐ Plan performance lineup and running order
  • ☐ Arrange sound system, lighting, and AV equipment
  • ☐ Hire DJ or live band
  • ☐ Coordinate decor, stage backdrop, seating, lighting, floral arrangements
  • ☐ Plan catering, dinner buffet, live counters, bar service
  • ☐ Hire emcee/host to manage event flow
  • ☐ Arrange rehearsal time for performers
  • ☐ Set up photo/video booth
  • ☐ Coordinate outfits for all performers
  • ☐ Hire professional photographer and videographer
  • ☐ Arrange transportation and valet parking
  • ☐ Prepare backup plans for technical issues

Haldi Ceremony, Blessing, Purification, and Laughter

🕉️ Tradition & Meaning

The Haldi ceremony is one of the most joyful, intimate, and photogenic pre-wedding rituals. Turmeric paste (haldi) mixed with sandalwood, rose water, and oils is applied to the bride and groom by family members and close friends. This ritual is believed to purify, bless, and beautify the couple before their wedding day.

What makes Haldi special is its playful, unscripted nature. It starts as a solemn blessing and inevitably turns into a turmeric fight, with everyone covered in yellow, laughing, and creating some of the most candid, joyful wedding photos. In Jharkhand, families often incorporate local elements like applying haldi with sal leaves or singing traditional folk songs during the ceremony.

📅 When & Duration

Held on the morning of the wedding day or one day before, the Haldi ceremony typically lasts 1-2 hours. It's usually a morning or early afternoon event, held separately at the bride's and groom's homes or at a common venue.

🎨 Decor Ideas for 2026

  • • Yellow Everywhere: Marigold flowers, yellow drapes, lemon and turmeric installations, embrace the vibrant yellow theme
  • • Outdoor Garden Setup: Natural sunlight, fresh flowers, and greenery create the perfect Instagram-worthy Haldi backdrop
  • • Boho Chic: Macramé, dreamcatchers, pampas grass, and pastel yellow accents for a modern aesthetic
  • • Traditional Jharkhand Touch: Brass vessels, terracotta pots, bamboo seating, and Sohrai art panels
  • • Photo-Worthy Seating: Decorated wooden pidi/chowki, floral swings, or low seating with colorful cushions
  • • Haldi Station: Beautifully arranged brass or copper bowls with turmeric paste, flowers, and rose petals

🎭 Entertainment & Atmosphere

The Haldi ceremony is intimate and playful. Background music includes devotional songs, folk music, or upbeat Bollywood tracks. Live dhol players add energy, and many families in Ranchi now hire photographers who specialize in capturing candid, joyful Haldi moments.

Haldi Ceremony Activities:

  • • Traditional haldi application by elders and family
  • • Playful turmeric fights among friends
  • • Folk songs and dhol performances
  • • Candid photography and videography
  • • Refreshments and light snacks for guests
  • • Flower shower and blessings

👗 What to Wear

Bride & Groom: Comfortable, yellow or white ethnic wear that you don't mind getting stained, kurta-pajama, simple suits, or traditional outfits. Floral jewelry is popular for brides.
Guests: Yellow, white, or pastel ethnic wear. Since turmeric stains, guests should wear clothes they're comfortable getting messy.

✅ Planning Checklist

  • ☐ Choose venue, home, garden, farmhouse, or banquet hall
  • ☐ Prepare haldi paste (turmeric, sandalwood, rose water, oils)
  • ☐ Arrange decor, flowers, drapes, seating, photo backdrop
  • ☐ Set up haldi application area with proper seating
  • ☐ Arrange brass/copper bowls and vessels for haldi
  • ☐ Hire photographer specializing in candid shots
  • ☐ Arrange dhol players or background music
  • ☐ Plan refreshments, light snacks, beverages, sweets
  • ☐ Coordinate yellow/white outfits for bride, groom, and family
  • ☐ Arrange towels, water, and cleanup facilities
  • ☐ Prepare guest list (typically 30-60 close family and friends)
  • ☐ Set up changing rooms for bride and groom post-ceremony
