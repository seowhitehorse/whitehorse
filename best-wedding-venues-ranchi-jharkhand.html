How to Choose the Right Wedding Venue: 8 Essential Questions

With so many beautiful options, how do you choose the right venue for your wedding? Here are the 8 most important questions to ask:

1. What's Your Guest Count?

Your guest list determines your venue options. Be realistic about numbers — venues feel best when they're appropriately filled, not half-empty or overcrowded.

2. What's Your Budget Per Plate?

Venue costs are typically calculated per plate (including food, service, and venue rental). Know your budget range before you start visiting venues.

3. Indoor, Outdoor, or Both?

Do you dream of an outdoor ceremony under the stars, or do you prefer the climate-controlled comfort of an indoor banquet hall? Many venues offer both options.

4. What's Your Wedding Season?

Ranchi's peak wedding season (October–February) requires booking 4–8 months in advance. Summer and monsoon weddings offer more availability and often better pricing.

5. Do You Need Guest Accommodation?

If you have outstation guests, venues with on-site rooms or nearby hotel partnerships make logistics much easier.

6. What's Included in the Package?

Clarify what's included: catering, décor, AV equipment, furniture, service staff, parking, power backup. Hidden costs add up quickly.

7. Can You Bring Your Own Vendors?

Some venues require you to use their in-house caterers and decorators. Others allow external vendors. Know the policy before booking.

8. What's the Cancellation & Refund Policy?

Life is unpredictable. Understand the venue's cancellation terms, refund policy, and date-change flexibility before signing the contract.

Pro Tips from White Horse Event Management

  • ✓ Visit During the Same Time of Day: If you're planning an evening wedding, visit the venue in the evening to see lighting, ambiance, and traffic conditions.
  • ✓ Check Parking & Accessibility: Ensure there's adequate parking and easy access for elderly guests and those with mobility challenges.
  • ✓ Ask About Power Backup: Ranchi experiences occasional power cuts. Confirm the venue has generator backup for uninterrupted celebrations.
  • ✓ Read Reviews: Check WedMeGood, Google Reviews, and TripAdvisor for honest feedback from couples who've celebrated there.
  • ✓ Negotiate Smartly: Venues often have flexibility on pricing, especially for off-season dates or weekday weddings.
  • ✓ Get Everything in Writing: Verbal promises don't hold up. Ensure all agreements — pricing, inclusions, policies — are documented in your contract.
